    Explore Kashmir

    Explore The Beauty of kashmir with us, At LandLife Tour and Travels, we understand that the Kashmir is full of incredible destinations, unique experiences, and memorable adventures

    Adventures to do in kashmir

    Things We'll Do

    Shikara Ride

    A shikara ride in Kashmir is a popular and iconic experience for tourists visiting the region. A shikara is a type of wooden boat, often handcrafted with intricate designs, that is commonly found on the picturesque Dal Lake in Srinagar, as well as other lakes and waterways in the Kashmir Valley.

    Houseboat Stay

    A houseboat stay on Dal Lake in Kashmir is a unique and popular accommodation option for tourists visiting the region. Dal Lake, located in Srinagar, is known for its stunning natural beauty and is surrounded by snow-capped mountains. Houseboats on Dal Lake offer visitors a one-of-a-kind experience, combining comfort and a close connection to the local culture and environment.

    Gondola Ride

    A gondola ride in Gulmarg, Kashmir, refers to the Gulmarg Gondola, which is one of the highest cable cars in the world. It's a popular tourist attraction that takes visitors from the base of Gulmarg to the higher altitudes of the Pir Panjal mountain range.

    River Rafting

    River rafting in Kashmir, particularly in Pahalgam and Sonamarg, is an exhilarating adventure activity that allows you to experience the natural beauty of the region while navigating through the thrilling and challenging waters of the Lidder River in Pahalgam and the Sindh River in Sonamarg.

    Paragliding

    Paragliding in Kashmir is a thrilling adventure activity that allows you to experience the scenic beauty of the region from the air. With its stunning landscapes, lush valleys, and majestic mountains, Kashmir provides an ideal backdrop for paragliding.

    Trekking

    Trekking in Kashmir is a popular adventure activity that allows you to explore the stunning natural beauty of the region, including the Himalayan mountains, lush valleys, and pristine alpine landscapes. Kashmir offers a variety of trekking opportunities, catering to both beginners and experienced trekkers.

    Skiing & Snowboarding

    Skiing and snowboarding in Kashmir are popular winter sports activities, especially in the region's scenic areas like Gulmarg.

    Kashmiri Wazwaan

    Kashmiri Wazwan is a traditional multi-course meal in the Kashmir Valley of India. It is renowned for its rich and aromatic dishes, as well as its association with the culture and heritage of the region. Wazwan is often served at special occasions, weddings, and other celebrations.
